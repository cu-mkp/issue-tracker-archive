Puys, si tu<lb/> veulx, fortifie le<lb/> de <tl><m>papier collé</m></tl><lb/> par derriere.</ab> ``` Which we have translated as: ``` Polished <tl><m><fr>carton</fr></m></tl> of little thickness &amp; a little moistened is proper. Then if you want, strengthen it with <tl><m><fr>papier collé</fr></m></tl> on the back.</ab> ``` I'm not sure why this "papier collé" is left in French as its own material.
