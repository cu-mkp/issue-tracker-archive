102: Determine translation of "métal" according to context

"Métal" is used in two sense in the ms. Search all instances and decide which it is: 1) generic sense, i.e. translate "metal; 2) specific sense: denoting a specific kind of metal, in contradistinction to cast iron. Likely a variety of bronze, as used in bells and petards (see DMF). In these cases, put an editorial comment to that effect: "Here the word "métal" refers not to metal in general, but to a specific kind of metal, in contradistinction to cast iron; likely a variety of bronze used for bells and petards (see DMF)."
