179: Translation/use of "comme"

What to do with “comme”? - MHS: in most cases, it means “as”; however, when it qualifies adjectives, the meaning is unclear. Sometimes, we use “somewhat,” in other cases, we use “as if.” - Can we find something consistent to use for this second instance (qualifying adj.)? - Check that ‘somewhat’ could work for ‘comme’ across the manuscript (see, for example, 121r for a clear sense of the word in this way) - do we need to do this separately from the translation refinement campaign?
