852: should we remove the <sup>of sulfur</sup> in 46v_6 and 46_7

On one hand, we want to get rid of <sup> tags as much as possible. On the other, this makes markup problematic, as the material will be "aforesaid oil" — will this get associated with "oil of sulfur" in the markup vocabulary normalization? NB: there is an editorial comment explaining that "aforesaid oil" is oil of sulfur.
