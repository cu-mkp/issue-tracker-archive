171: Meaning/translation of “foeille de topasse”

what does “foeille de topasse” mean? - on 102v “foeille de topasse, d’or ou d’argente”; - on fol. 120r: “lames de foeilles de pierrerie” - PHS 2018: I think “foeilles de pierrerie” means the leaves of metal (foils?) that form backings for gems, so “lames de...” means sheets of these metal backing leaves.
