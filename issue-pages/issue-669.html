669: translation policy on "grain"

**GLOSSARY:** Grain = keep Fr., as a unit of measurement “Unité d’évaluation de la pureté de l’argent et de l’or” (DMF) (SM). **PROBLEM:** Only 7 instances of "grain" have been left in French in the TL. All of them occur when "grain" is used as a measurement (and thus, they have `<ms>` tags as well) and has something to do with metal. However, there are other instances of "grain" used as a measurement that have NOT been kept in French (e.g., 116v: "throw in two or three `<ms>`grains`</ms>` of `<m>`rosin`</m>`" or 104r: "about four `<ms>`grains`</ms>` of `<m>`sal ammoniac`</m>`, &amp; six `<ms>`grains`</ms>` of `<m>`sublimate`</m>`") **ISSUES TO RESOLE** 1.
