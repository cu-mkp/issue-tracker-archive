190: translation of “lien”

More information required to make decision about translation of “lien” - tied bundle? - current vocab entry: Lien, “à lien” = keep in french with `<fr>` tags. A unit of measure describing a quantity for sale. Cotgrave calls a "lien" a cord, but on f. 20r the MS describes a "lien" as composed of three "tables" of glass. (SM and MBR)
