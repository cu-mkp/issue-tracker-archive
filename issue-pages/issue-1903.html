1903: remove period from p133r_3 header in tcn

The tcn of 133r_3 ends with a period although the author-practitioner does not write one. Protocol states that no headers end in a period unless provided by the A-P. Remove this period: `<head>Marques des <tl>poinctes de <m>fil de fer</m></tl><lb/>qui se trouvent en la teste de l’animal.</head>`
