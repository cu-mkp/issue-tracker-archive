If there is more than one entry in the "alternate-spellings" column of the glossary, they are currently separated by a | These should now all be changed to a comma. For example, now: head-word | alternate-spellings -- | -- Aguiser | aiguiser \| esguiser change to: head-word | alternate-spellings -- | -- Aguiser | aiguiser, esguiser NOTE: this also requires the removal of an extra space. This would thus render in the DCE as: CURRENT: Aguiser, aiguiser | esguiser (mod. aiguiser): v. to sharpen BETTER: Aguiser, aiguiser, esguiser (mod. aiguiser): v. to sharpen
