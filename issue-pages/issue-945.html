There are many pseudonymous "secrets of Albertus magnus" published from the late 14th century on, and they may well contain this recipe, but this note is not a scholarly reference, altho it is kind of useful info for the person who wants to explore further. What to do? Any thoughts? My different scholarly and information-loving instincts are at war here....
