208: Indicate ms structural markers of continuation, etc

Structural markers of continuation etc., such as “This is the continuation of the second preceding leaf,” on fol. 146v need to be indicated the DCE in some way. These are not consistently identified or flagged. Some exist as GD comments (but inconsistently) and others in Composition & structure of MS for a handlist of other such structural markers; still others are not marked at all Need to figure out how to render this structure in a systematic way. DECISION: Editorial comment for now - for all instances ideally. Desirable but not priority for now. How much time/energy to allow for this?
