803: Inconsistent accents in TCN

What precisely is the normalisation protocol regarding accents? My understanding was that they were only to be used for disambiguation, e.g. aprés vs. âpres, chassés vs chasses, etc. However, meslée, for example, consistently has an accent (except one instance where we added the accent to be consistent), and we cannot see what this could be confused with. Also, joinctée occurs twice with an accent, once, without. Also see potée: 6 with accents, 2 without, and one pottée.
