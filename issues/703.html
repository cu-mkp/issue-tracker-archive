#703: "verd de terre" and "vert de terre"

opened by thuchacz

PROBLEM Are "vert de terre" and "verd de terre" actually different terms?

Occurrences: "verd de terre" in 135r3 "vert de terre" in p009v1, p129r_2

GLOSSARY ENTRIES: - Verd/vert de terre = a color, in Cotgrave “ a kind of greene minerall chalke”, keep in french. (SD HW 7/10/18) - Vert de terre = “green verditer”, i.e. an artificial basic copper carbonate The Grove Encyclopedia of Materials and Technique in Art, 506
