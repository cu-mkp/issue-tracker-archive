opened by thuchacz

roquaille: n.(f) rocaille

Current glossary entry: "rocaille (in Ms. Fr. 640, this refers to small glass beads which may or may not be colored)"

New glossary entry: "rocaille is a term for small glass beads, which could be tinted. For enameling, those with least color were normally recommended for use."

Glossary entry for rocaille now reads: rocaille is a term for small glass beads, which could be colorless or tinted. For enameling, those with least color were normally recommended for use.
