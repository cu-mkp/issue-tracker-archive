PROBLEM Very inconsistent translation of "sayes" and "sayettes"

GLOSSARY WORK @TillmannTaape has just made a new glossary entry for "saye" and updated "sayette":

  • Saye 1 small bristle brush used by goldsmiths
  • Saye 2 light wool fabric known as "say" in English, or a garment made of such fabric
  • Sayette very small bristle brush (diminutive of <i>saye</i>, i.e. a small bristle brush used by goldsmiths)
