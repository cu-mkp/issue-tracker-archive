#1231: Curate metadata for annotation "front matter"

opened by njr2128

The following information will be included as a "front matter" header at the top(?) of each page of individual essay (not necessarily in this order):

  • Essay Title
  • Folios link
  • "Back to" list
  • Categories (both thematic and entry category)
  • Type (research or introductory)
  • Author's name(s)
  • Author demographic info (i.e., affiliation at time of authorship, degree/year if students)
  • Essay "last updated" date (to establish the last time edits were made, particularly for quotes of the tc or tcn)
  • Author disclaimer
  • Cite as info (including doi)

This information needs to be curated and added to metadata for Nick to render this (execute work for https://github.com/cu-mkp/making-knowing-edition/issues/234)
