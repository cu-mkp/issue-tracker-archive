#1597: Fol. 52r - misplaced parenthesis in translation

opened by ps2270

Fol. 52r - Diplomatic: "(germe) voy)"

TN: le germe (voy)

TL: the germ (see)

Shouldn't TN and TL mirror TC?? We don't know what the a-p meant, so why try to interpret this?
