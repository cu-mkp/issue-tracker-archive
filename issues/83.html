#83: translate "lumière" as “touch-hole” or “sight” depending on context

opened by TillmannTaape

lumière = “touch-hole” in the context of gunnery (handguns as well as cannon etc.); “sight” in the context of aiming a gun. In some places it has been incorrectly translated as “fuse” rather than “touch-hole” – check throughout.
